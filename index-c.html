Concept

august - sep 2020

Problem

People want to throw clothing but they don’t have time and not many places to dispose.

Vision

Vision: 20% of all CO2 emission is from activity related to clothing. We want solve problem related to SGD goal 12: reponsible consumption of clothing




Solution

august - sept 2020

A platform for bulk clothing exchange for Tokyo dwellersplatform for bulk


Personalized shopping experience

AI & algorithm helps the user find the best matching clothing in size and style. No more trouble with finding a secondhand-clothing that fits

Exchange all at once

Quick exchange for user who wants to have all their old clothing gone quickly. Exchange clothing in batch of 10 feature helps user to save time!

Budget and Environmentally friendly

Spending less on clothes while reducing impact on the environment by using secondhand clothing.




Progress

oct - dec 2020


Design

Re:clo is designed with an emphasis on the user’s experience. User would feel like using a secondhand clothing website but having a fun shopping experience.


Front & Back-end

The format of the platform is Progressive Web-application. It works best on smartphone platform and hence is convenient for casual user. The function includes in the application are: Dashboard, Shopping cart & Order function, Upload function.


AI & Algorithm

AI & algorithm are used to make the shopping and upload process smarter and faster. The first function is to automatically classify clothes into categories. The second function is to recommend clothing items to shopper based on matching body size and age.
